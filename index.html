ZimAlert – Child Safety Standards & Protection Policy

ZimAlert is committed to protecting children and preventing any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Our platform strictly prohibits:

  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming, trafficking, or exploitation
  • Sharing or requesting explicit images
  • Any behavior that endangers children

Our Safety Measures

  • Strict content moderation
  • Immediate account suspension for violations
  • Reporting tools for harmful content
  • Cooperation with law enforcement

Reporting Abuse
